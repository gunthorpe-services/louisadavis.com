About Louisa

Hello, I'm Louisa

I'm a qualified counsellor with a passion for supporting people through some of life's most challenging moments. My approach is person-centred — meaning I follow your lead, working at a pace that feels right for you.

I trained at the British Association for Counselling & Psychotherapy (BACP) and hold a Diploma in Counselling. I work entirely online, which means sessions fit around your life — no commute, no waiting rooms, just a quiet space wherever you feel most comfortable.

I believe that being truly heard can be the first step towards feeling better. If you're curious about whether counselling might help, I'd love to have a conversation.

  • BACP Diploma in Counselling
  • Registered BACP Member
  • DBS Checked
  • Fully insured & confidential

What I Offer

How I can help

Counselling can help with a wide range of difficulties. Here are some of the areas I specialise in — though I welcome any concern you'd like to bring.

🌿

Anxiety & Stress

Persistent worry, panic attacks, overwhelm, or a constant sense of dread — together we'll explore what's driving your anxiety and build strategies to find calm.

🌤

Depression & Low Mood

Feeling flat, empty, or unable to enjoy things you once loved. I'll work with you gently to understand what's happened and help you reconnect with a sense of hope.

🤝

Relationships & Life Changes

Relationship difficulties, bereavement, divorce, career transitions, or any significant change. Sometimes life asks more of us than we feel ready to give — I can help you find your footing.
